About Us

WaterWise: Water Management Solution

At WaterWise, we harness the power of social media data and cutting-edge technology to address water-related challenges in disaster and crisis management. Our innovative approach leverages geo-referenced images shared by citizens to provide situational awareness to emergency responders and assist with financial loss assessment during flooding events, water quality issues, urban flooding, and drainage problems.

  • Community engagement for local empowerment.
  • Data-driven solutions for effective problem-solving.
  • Advocacy to drive policy change and awareness.
  • Innovation in technology for improved water management.
  • Collaboration with stakeholders for collective impact.
